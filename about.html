About Sweet Treats Bakery

Our Story

Welcome to Sweet Treats Bakery, where passion for baking meets the joy of creating delicious treats. Founded in 2010 by Maria Gonzalez, our bakery started as a small family business in Johannesburg with a simple mission: to bake fresh, high-quality goods using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Over the years, we've grown from a tiny shop to a beloved local institution, serving the community with love and dedication. Our team of skilled bakers uses only the finest ingredients, ensuring every bite is a moment of pure bliss.

At Sweet Treats Bakery, we believe that baking is not just about food—it's about creating memories. Whether it's a birthday cake for your child, pastries for a morning meeting, or cupcakes for a celebration, we're here to make your occasions sweeter.

Come visit us and experience the warmth of our bakery. We're committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly packaging and sourcing ingredients locally whenever possible.
